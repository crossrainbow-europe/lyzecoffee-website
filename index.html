Our story我们的故事

Industrial soul,
blue heart 工业灵魂，
蓝色之心

We seek out exceptional specialty coffees from around the world and roast each one with meticulous care — so that anyone, not just the connoisseur, can taste the difference and fall in love with what coffee can be.

我们寻找世界各地优质的精品咖啡，精心烘焙每一支豆子，让每个人都能轻松感受咖啡的丰富风味。

We also believe coffee doesn't have to stand alone. By pairing it with carefully chosen ingredients — fruits, spices, botanicals — we explore new flavor harmonies that surprise and delight.

我们将咖啡与各种食材相结合，探索出令人惊喜的全新风味组合。

Our academy — rooted in the heritage of 露元咖啡培训学院 (First Drop) — brings the same commitment to education, led by a licensed Q Instructor and Chinese Coffee League brewing judge. Opening soon.

我们的咖啡学院传承自露元咖啡培训学院，由持牌 Q Instructor 及中国咖啡联赛冲煮评审执裁主理。即将开业。

Coffee Academy咖啡学院

Learn from
the best向最好的
学习

Our academy is a place where passion for specialty coffee becomes mastery. Whether you're a curious beginner or an aspiring competition barista — this is where the journey deepens.

咖啡学院是热爱转化为专业的地方。无论你是初学者还是志在竞技的咖啡师，这里都是深耕咖啡之道的起点。

🏅
SCA Licensed Q Instructor Certified to train and certify Q Graders — one of the most advanced coffee credentials in the world, and rare in China. 持牌 Q Instructor，具备培训并认证 Q Grader 的资质，此资质在中国极为稀缺。
⚖️
中国咖啡联赛冲煮评审执裁 Licensed competition judge for brewing events under the Chinese Coffee League (CCL). 中国咖啡联赛冲煮赛项官方认证评审执裁。

Rooted in the legacy of 露元咖啡培训学院 (First Drop Coffee Academy), with years of training experience and a deep connection to China's specialty coffee competition scene.

传承自露元咖啡培训学院，深耕中国精品咖啡培训与竞技多年。
