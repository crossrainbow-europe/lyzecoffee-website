Our story我们的故事

Industrial soul,
blue heart 工业灵魂，
蓝色之心

We seek out exceptional specialty coffees from around the world and roast each one with meticulous care — so that anyone, not just the connoisseur, can taste the difference and fall in love with what coffee can be.

我们寻找世界各地优质的精品咖啡，精心烘焙每一支豆子，让每个人都能轻松感受咖啡的丰富风味。

We also believe coffee doesn't have to stand alone. By pairing it with carefully chosen ingredients — fruits, spices, botanicals — we explore new flavor harmonies that surprise and delight.

我们将咖啡与各种食材相结合，探索出令人惊喜的全新风味组合。

Our academy — rooted in the heritage of First Drop Coffee Academy — brings the same commitment to education, led by a licensed Q Instructor and Chinese Coffee League brewing judge.

我们的咖啡学院传承自露元咖啡培训学院，由持牌 Q Instructor 及中国咖啡联赛冲煮评审执裁主理。即将开业。
